Established in 2024 and conveniently located at 1480D Dominion Road, Mount Roskill, Auckland 1041. MOON Takeaway is proud to serve fresh, delicious meals to our local community. We are open 6 days a week from 11:00 AM to 9:00 PM, making it easy to enjoy your favorite dishes whenever you're craving great food.

Every meal is prepared with care using quality ingredients to ensure a satisfying dining experience. At MOON Takeaway, we specialize in

  • classic Fish & Chips
  • homemade hamburgers
  • crispy chicken nibbles
  • and a variety of flavorful stir-fried dishes.

Our owner and head chef bring many years of cooking experience and a passion for creating food that customers love. Thanks to our commitment to quality, taste, and friendly service, we have earned numerous 5-star reviews and the trust of our valued customers.

We look forward to welcoming you and serving food that keeps you coming back for more.
